🔒 Privacy Policy

Summary: WatchTool collects ZERO personal data. All your Watch Later data stays only in your browser. We never see your YouTube account, email, or any personal information.

1. Who We Are

WatchTool is an independent Chrome extension that enhances YouTube's Watch Later playlist. It is NOT affiliated with Google, YouTube, or any third party.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect ZERO personal data. Specifically:

  • ❌ We do NOT collect your YouTube username or account info
  • ❌ We do NOT collect your email address
  • ❌ We do NOT track your YouTube browsing or watch history
  • ❌ We do NOT run analytics or tracking pixels
  • ❌ We do NOT send data to any server (free or Pro)
  • ❌ We do NOT see your Google credentials or session tokens

3. Payment Processing

If you purchase Pro features, payment is handled by LemonSqueezy (third-party processor):

  • We receive ONLY a payment confirmation (you upgraded)

4. What Data Stays in Your Browser

Data Type Stored Where Purpose Can You Delete?
Video metadata (title, duration, channel) Browser (IndexedDB) Search & filtering ✓ Yes - clear extension data
User settings (sort, filters, preferences) Browser (chrome.storage.sync) Remember your settings ✓ Yes - clear extension data
First-seen dates for videos Browser (chrome.storage.local) Detect stale/old videos ✓ Yes - clear extension data

Important: All data above stays ONLY on your device. Nothing is ever sent to us.

5. Third-Party Services

WatchTool uses:

  • YouTube: We interact with the YouTube page you're already on (no API calls)
  • LemonSqueezy: Payment processing only (if you buy Pro)
  • NO analytics, NO tracking pixels, NO ad networks
⚠️ Pro Feature Note: The AI topic grouping feature (Pro only) sends ONLY video titles to an AI API for clustering. No personal data, no account info. You can disable this and use local processing instead.

6. YouTube Integration

WatchTool reads and interacts with YouTube's web page exactly like you would manually:

  • ✓ Works with videos already in your Watch Later

  • ✓ Does NOT use YouTube's official API
  • ✓ Does NOT authenticate with Google on your behalf

📥 Installation & Support

How to Install

  1. Go to Chrome Web Store and search "WatchTool"
  2. Click "Add to Chrome" — review permissions (youtube.com only)
  3. Go to youtube.com/playlist?list=WL (your Watch Later)
  4. Click the WatchTool icon in your toolbar to start
  5. Optional: Pin WatchTool to your toolbar for quick access

Compatibility

Troubleshooting

  • Extension stopped working? YouTube updates can break extensions. We fix within 24-48 hours.
  • Want to uninstall? Right-click WatchTool icon → "Remove from Chrome". Your YouTube Watch Later is unaffected.
  • Issues persist? Try: refresh page (Ctrl+R) → disable other extensions → check Chrome is updated

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Does WatchTool access my Google account?

❌ No. WatchTool does NOT authenticate with Google. It reads the YouTube page you're already logged into—exactly like you would manually.

Will it get my YouTube account banned?

❌ No. WatchTool only clicks YouTube's buttons at normal human speed (2-3 actions/second). It's identical to manual clicking.

Where does my data go?

🔒 Nowhere. All data (settings, video titles, stale detection dates) stays in your browser's local storage. Zero data leaves your device.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

✓ Yes. Your settings sync via chrome.storage.sync across devices on the same Chrome profile. Video metadata stays local per device.

How do I cancel Pro?

Log into your LemonSqueezy account (from your purchase email) → Subscriptions → Cancel. Pro access continues until billing period ends.

Does bulk delete remove videos from YouTube?

✓ Yes—from your Watch Later only. Videos still exist on YouTube. You can re-add them anytime.

What happens if I uninstall?

All WatchTool data (settings, cache, licence key) is deleted. Your YouTube Watch Later is completely unaffected.

Does it work with Brave/Safari/Firefox?

⚠️ Brave might work (disable Shields for YouTube). Safari and Firefox use different extension systems—not supported yet.
