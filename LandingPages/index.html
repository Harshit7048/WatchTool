The problem

YouTube's Watch Later
is a graveyard.

You save videos with great intentions. Then they vanish into a list you can't search, can't sort, can't clean. Sound familiar?

01
No search inside your list
Saved 600 videos? Good luck finding that React tutorial from 8 months ago. YouTube gives you no search, no filter — just infinite scroll.
02
Deleting one video at a time
Three clicks per video. Have 200 to clean out? That's 600 clicks. There is no "select all", no "delete from this channel", nothing.
03
The 5,000 video wall
"Maximum playlist size reached." YouTube silently stops saving your videos. You have no way to expand it — until now.
04
Videos from 2 years ago
Half your list is outdated content you'll never watch. No way to see what's old, no way to clean up stale saves. It just keeps growing.
05
No order, no priority
Can't put "watch next" at the top. Can't reorder. YouTube shows everything in saved order and you can't change it without removing and re-adding.
06
Algorithm poisoning
Binge one genre and your entire recommendation feed shifts. No way to separate "work research" videos from "3am curiosity" saves.
